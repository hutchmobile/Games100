WHO WE ARE

Pradeep Constructions established in the year 1989., brings to you a wholesome, premium and sophisticated way of living. It is an address of pomp and splendour that you'd be proud to own. Recognised as a household name for being one of the best for quality construction and timely deliveries, Pradeep Constructions is evinced as an astute leader of the Real Estate sector in Hyderabad.

At every project, Pradeep Constructions imperatively incorporates sizeable open spaces and green areas to keep the ecosystem intact providing vibrant living for their habitants. They have successfully completed numerous high-profile projects including one of Hyderabad’s first government approved IT Parks at Banjara Hills, apart from adorning the busy streets of Somajiguda , Ameerpet & Hitech City with their impeccable structures. Pradeep Constructions is all set and equipped to extend its excellence, and opened its doors to tie-ups with multiple larger firms.
