About Us

Pradeep Constructions brings to you a wholesome, premium and luxurious way of life and living. It is an address of pomp and splendour that you'd be proud to own for a lifetime and beyond. With remarkable prominence and infrastructure, Pradeep Constructions, established in 1989 has been the leader assuring happiness in cement mix for the last 30 years! Recognised and received as one of the best for quality construction and timely delivery, Pradeep Constructions is an evinced thought leader of the Real Estate sector in Hyderabad.
Every project imperatively incorporates sizeable open spaces and green areas to provide quality life spaces enhancing living offering desirable lifestyles. Our successful completion of a number of high-profile projects including one of Hyderabad’s first government approved IT Parks and, one of the city’s first high-end super-deluxe apartment complexes, and two landmark buildings in the busy streets of Ameerpet, all add glory to the astound expertise and excellence we possess. Today, Pradeep Constructions is all set and equipped to extend its excellence and presence in other Indian cities, opening doors to tie-ups with multiple larger firms.
